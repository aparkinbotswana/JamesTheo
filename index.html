Why you should hire me?

I’m a motivated and dedicated front-end web developer who loves having a nice balance of problem solving, logical thought and creativity in my work. I love programming and the feeling of achievement and satisfaction you get when your work finally starts taking shape. I hate programming and the endless frustration that comes with it. This is largely due to my interest with the Leap Motion and animation libraries such as p5.js and three.js. Technologies like these can create a fantastic user experience and I look forward to experimenting with them and learning more about similar technologies as my career continues. Ultimately, I hope to find myself in an environment that will challenge me and help me learn more about coding.

Leap Motion Virtual Auslan Interpreter

The Auslan Interpreter is a JavaScript app, designed to help those who are unable to understand Australian Sign Language, communicate with those who can. The app uses the Leap Motion to interpret hand gestures from the user. The program recognises the gesture and displays, as well as speaks the recognised letter.
