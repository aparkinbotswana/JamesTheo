Leap Motion Virtual Auslan Interpreter

The Auslan Interpreter is a JavaScript app, designed to help those who are unable to understand Australian Sign Language, communicate with those who can. The app uses the Leap Motion to interpret hand gestures from the user. The program recognises the gesture and displays, as well as speaks the recognised letter.
